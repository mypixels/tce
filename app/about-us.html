About Us

Welcome to TheCuttingEdge

Here at TheCuttingEdge we have been styling hair, in Arnold, for 14 years. In this time we have built an incredible reputation for service, hospitality and of course fantastic hair cuts, along with colour and fashion colour. Our team consists of only top stylists who have trained with some of the best in the world of hairdressing. Our salon operates over two floors including our beauty room, where you can relax and treat yourself to luxury facials etc and then have your hair professionally styled, all under one roof. We also offer a retail service where you can purchase the products that we use in the salon for your use at home.

Pop in for a free consulation, we look forward to seeing you!
